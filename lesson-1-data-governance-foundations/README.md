# Lesson 1: Data Governance Foundations

## Overview

This lesson covers the fundamentals of establishing an AI data governance framework, including governance charters, stakeholder mapping, communication plans, and committee structures. Students learn to build a governance foundation that aligns AI initiatives with organizational goals and regulatory requirements.

## Exercise

**Scenario: MedTech AI Corp** — A healthcare company (2,000 employees) deploying TriageAssist (emergency triage support) and ClinicalNoteAI (clinical documentation AI).

### Task

Using the MedTech AI Corp scenario, students complete four governance deliverables:

1. **Governance Charter** — Define the organization's AI governance vision, mission, scope, FATE principles (Fairness, Accountability, Transparency, Explainability), and decision-making authority.
2. **Stakeholder Mapping** — Identify key stakeholders across clinical, technical, compliance, and executive roles. Assign influence levels, interests, and engagement strategies.
3. **Communication Plan** — Design a communication cadence for different stakeholder audiences, specifying channels, frequency, and responsible owners.
4. **Committee Structure** — Establish an AI governance committee with defined roles, meeting cadence, escalation paths, and decision authority.
